Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

SudokuAF respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

SudokuAF does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Tracking data

2. Data Storage

All game data (such as current puzzle progress and settings) is stored locally on your device.

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

SudokuAF does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or external services.

4. Internet Access

SudokuAF does not require an internet connection to function.

5. Children's Privacy

Because SudokuAF does not collect any data, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.

6. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

SudokuAF is designed to be simple, private, and distraction-free.

No ads. No tracking. No nonsense.
